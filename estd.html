ABOUT SCHOOL


ESTABLISHMENT

Khawbung Senior Secondary School was established in 2023 (Memo No. B. 11013/19/2019 - DSE(E)) and it is managed by Samagra Shiksha (RMSA). It is located in Vanzau block of Champhai District, Mizoram. It consists of Grades from 11 to 12 (Science Stream). English is the medium of instructions in this school. The total student strength is 30.

  As we look towards the future, Khawbung Senior Secondary School remains committed to providing its students with the tools and resources they need to succeed, shaping them into the leaders of tomorrow.

  We offer the following subjects :

  1. English

  2. Mizo (MIL)

  3. Physics

  4. Chemistry

  5. Biology

  6. Mathematics

Science Stream

The Science Stream after completion of Class 10 is quite a popular choice in India. English, Physics and Chemistry are compulsory, students can take up any optional subject to meet a total of five (5) subjects.

  • Physics
